# PROFILE — <your name>

**This file is yours. It lives outside the plugin, so it is never packaged, never shared with
whoever gave you this, and never committed to a public repo.** It holds the personal facts the
engine needs to place work where your actual body and actual life can do it.

**Be precise about what that does and does not mean, because the comfortable version is a lie.**
This file does **not** ship with the plugin. It **does** pass through the model on every run —
exactly like your mail and your calendar already do, under your AI provider's terms. **"Not
packaged" is not "never leaves your machine."**

**So apply one test to every line: would you send this fact through a cloud service?** If no,
leave it out. **The engine runs fine without any single field** — a blank line costs you a little
precision; a fact you regret costs you more. Nothing here is mandatory except the offense lanes,
and even those you can decline.

**Why it exists as a separate file:** the law is universal and portable; *you* are not. Keeping
you out of the law is what lets the law be shared, and keeping the law out of you is what lets
your facts be corrected without editing a rulebook.

**Fill this in with the setup skill rather than alone** — it will interview you.

**Keep it to durable facts.** If a line would go stale in six months, it belongs on a task row's
description instead, beside the loop it governs — not here. *(Test: could this become wrong
tomorrow without anyone touching this file? Then it doesn't belong here.)*

## Offense lanes — the most important section, and the one nothing else can supply

**The work that is yours, that you actually want, that has nobody chasing it.** The venture. The
book. The portfolio. The thing you'd say you're really trying to do.

**Why this section exists and why it can't be inferred:** the engine's main filter only admits
things where someone is waiting on you and a date breaks. Your life's work has neither. **If you
leave this blank, the engine physically cannot see your own work**, and it will hand you a clean,
competent card every morning composed entirely of other people's demands — and you will not
notice, because it will look like it's working.

**You declare these. The engine never guesses.** Guessing what matters in your life is the one
